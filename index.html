traditional food

　Korea is famous for its mustardy food to warm up the body. I will now introduce three traditional Korean foods.　

samgyetang

　　A dish of chicken stuffed with carrots, glutinous rice, and pine nuts. Good for beauty and health.It is said that if you eat it from July to August, you will stay free from illness for a year.　

Mando

　It is similar to dumplings in Japan. It is a popular snack for the common people. It is made by kneading wheat flour or buckwheat flour into a skin with meat, tofu, kimchi, vegetables, etc.　

japchae

　Stir-fry and dress a variety of ingredients such as Korean vermicelli tangmyong, carrots, mushrooms, and shredded beef. It is seasoned with a soy sauce base and has the aroma of sesame oil. An indispensable menu item for celebrations in Korea since ancient times.
