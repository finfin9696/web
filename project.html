Manners

Unlike Japan, Korea has a manner of not lifting a vessel and having a buggy one-handed knee when sitting.If you hold a small bowl, it will become a cutting board, so attach your left and left hands to a plate and eat with chopsticks or a spoon. In Korea, if you eat all of your food without leaving any leftovers, you will be considered to have not eaten enough. So it's okay to leave a little. As you can see, there are some cases where it is considered rude in Japan, but good manners in Korea.

Weather

Korea is relatively cold compared to Japan.

In spring and autumn, there is little rain and it is relatively swollen. In January, the morning temperature may reach -7 ° C. The first snowfall was observed in Seoul on December 10, 2021.　Like Japan, it has four distinct seasons. Summer temperatures are not that different in both. In Korea, it rains twice as much in summer as in Japan.
